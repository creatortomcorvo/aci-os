  - CONFIRMED LISTED
  - NOT IDENTIFIED ON THE SOURCE CHECKED
  - NOT YET VERIFIED - DO NOT TREAT AS CLEARED
  - SOURCE UNAVAILABLE - DO NOT TREAT AS CLEARED
- Management/PEP/reputation review belongs in the main analysis unless the user explicitly asks for a separate appendix.
- OpenSanctions can be a final cross-check or secondary support, not an official source.
