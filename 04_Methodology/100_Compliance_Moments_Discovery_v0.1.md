# 100 Compliance Moments Discovery v0.1

Source: Tom's 2026-07-04 strategic note on "the 100 situations that make a compliance officer reach for their phone."

## Status

This is now the next methodology discovery step before building another expert route.

It does not replace the ABAC route. It defines the real-world situations that ABAC, sanctions, investigations, speak-up, competition, governance, and other routes must serve.

## Core Thesis

ACI-OS should not be built around the 100 regulations.

It should be built around the 100 moments.

The product wins if it understands the urgent, messy, human situations that make a compliance officer, Legal Counsel wearing Compliance Hat, GC, HR leader, or small-team compliance operator reach for help.

## Why This Comes Before Another Route

Building one more expert route too early risks creating a compliance encyclopedia.

Mapping the 100 moments first creates a practical demand map:

- What situations occur under pressure?

- Which response mode does the situation require before expert routing?
- What does the user need in the first 30 seconds?
- Which program elements are implicated?
- Which expert routes are needed?
- Which questions must be asked first?
- What must not be done?
- Who must be involved?
- What decision record should exist?

This is methodology work, so it fits the rule: Methodology before Product before Brand.

## Definition Of A Moment

A compliance moment is a concrete situation with pressure, uncertainty, and a possible wrong first move.

It is not a regulation, policy topic, or abstract risk category.

Good moment examples:

- Customs stopped our shipment.
- CEO wants an answer before lunch.
- We received an anonymous allegation.
- A salesperson invited a minister to a football match.
- A bank rejected our payment due to sanctions.
- Competition authority officials are at reception.
- HR wants to dismiss someone who recently reported harassment.
- A distributor wants a 15 percent success fee for consulting services.
- A journalist is asking about our agent in a high-risk country.
- A country manager says everyone pays facilitation fees there.

Weak moment examples:

- Anti-bribery policy.
- Sanctions regulation.
- Whistleblowing.
- Training.
- Risk assessment.

Those are domains. The product should start from moments and route to domains.

## Moment Template

Each moment should be captured in this structure:

1. Moment title.
2. User wording: how the user might actually say it.
3. Stress level: low, medium, high, or critical.
4. Pressure type: time, business, regulator, senior management, personal accountability, employee, public, financial, operational, or evidence.
5. Likely risk domains: sanctions, ABAC, speak-up, investigations, HR/retaliation, competition, data, governance, third party, trade/customs, finance controls, media, regulator.

5A. Response mode: Crisis, Judgment call, Counsel-boundary, Program self-assessment, or Escalation-mandatory.
6. Program elements: one or more of the 7-element backbone; add 12-element Annex A calibration if useful.
7. First 30 seconds: what the app should say first.
8. First 3-5 questions.
9. Do not do list.
10. Who to involve.
11. Decision or evidence record needed.
12. Likely expert route.
13. Article or training potential.

## Professional Navigation Output Concept

For each moment, ACI-OS should eventually provide a navigation envelope before deeper advice:

- Situation understood.
- Stress level: low / medium / high / critical.
- You are probably dealing with: regulatory risk, personal accountability, time pressure, business pressure, evidence risk, retaliation risk, or public exposure.
- This likely requires: CEO discussion, due diligence review, Legal involvement, HR involvement, Internal Audit, external counsel, board escalation, or provider/system check.
- Estimated decision effort: minutes, hours, days, or formal process.
- Confidence / uncertainty: qualitative or evidence-based, not fake precision.
- First safe step.
- What not to do.

Avoid fake precision. A number such as 82 percent should not be used unless the product later has a defined scoring model. For now use: high / medium / low confidence, and explain why.


Mode selection should be based on 04_Methodology/Question_Mode_Router_v0.1.md. The response mode decides answer shape before expert routing decides content.
