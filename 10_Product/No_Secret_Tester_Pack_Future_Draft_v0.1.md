# No-Secret Tester Pack - Future Draft v0.1

Status: placeholder for future external testing package.

## Purpose

This file remembers the required external-testing guardrails. It is not yet the tester pack itself.

## Required Components

A future no-secret tester pack should include:

1. One-page concept note.
2. Intended-use statement.
3. User-facing notice that this is a specialized compliance decision-support prototype, not general chat.
4. Prototype disclaimer.
5. No-secret / no-live-matter rules.
6. Pattern-first input instructions: describe roles and risk facts, not names or identifiers.
7. Not legal advice / not clearance wording.
8. No whistleblowing-channel / no investigation-tool wording.
9. Human-supervision requirement.
10. 15-25 synthetic test scenarios.
11. Feedback form.
12. Version number and date.
13. Contact route for questions.

## Mandatory Tester Warnings

- Do not enter company secrets.
- Describe patterns, roles, and risk facts; do not enter names, case IDs, customer names, contract numbers, or other identifiers.
- Do not enter personal data.
- Do not enter whistleblower identities.
- Do not enter live investigation facts.
- Do not enter privileged material.
- Do not test live sanctions, harassment, fraud, bribery, whistleblowing, employment, regulator, or law-enforcement matters.
- Do not rely on outputs as legal advice or company clearance.
- Escalate real issues through the company's approved Legal, Compliance, HR, privacy, Internal Audit, or external counsel channels.
